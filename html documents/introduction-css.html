Jinja Regional Referral Hospital

INTRODUCTION

Background

JRRH was found as a camping site for treating wounded soldiers during WWII, overtime it started treating other non-combat patients becoming a regional hospital. Now it stands tall on 9.6acres and serving a great number of districts in the Eastern region.

DEPARTMENTS IN THE HOSPITAL

Pharmacy Department

The Pharmacy Department is responsible for medicine supply chain in the hospital, storing and dispensing medicines. It is also responsible for pharmacovigilance, prescription audits and point prevelance studies.

The pharmacy department is also responsible for maximazing patient outcomes by ensuring that the right drugs are given to the right patients in their right doses with the right frequency and enough clear instructions to the patients.
